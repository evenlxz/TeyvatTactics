Alhaitham Guide

Written by: Evenlxz

General Info

Alhaitham is a 5-star geo sword character in Genshin Impact. His kit focuses on dealing off-field geo damage through his elemental skill. Alhaitham's skill, when placed, generates a burst of geo damage every 2 seconds when an enemy takes damage. Alhaitham's skill lasts a whopping 30 seconds, meaning it's not neccessary to switch back to him to continue damaging enemies. His burst does an instance of geo damage and buffs your teams elemental mastery. This makes Alhaitham a flexible damage dealer that can support most characters through off-field damage, geo-resonance, and elemental mastery buffing.

Pros & Cons

Pros

- Completely off-field damage tied to elemental skill.

- Flexible team building. Alhaitham can be put anywhere.

- Synergy with Zhongli/Chiori for geo-resonance.

- Elemental mastery buff on burst.

Cons

- Elemental skill can be easily destroyed by certain bosses.

- Potentially lower damage output than other support options.

- Needs at least geo-resonance to start doing noticable damage

- Generally overshadowed by Chiori.

How to Play

Alhaitham is simple character to play. Simply place your elemental skill and switch off him. If you are playing a team that can make use of elemental mastery, you should use his burst after his skill.

Be careful around large enemies or bosses, as placing your elemental skill inside of their models will cause the flower to instantly be destroyed. You can remedy this by placing Alhaitham's skill behind him, or holding his skill to get more precise placement.

Combo: Elemental Skill -> Elemental Burst (If required)

Be aware that Alhaitham's skill DOES snapshot. Cast any buffs you want him before casting his skill.

Weapons

Uraku Misugiri

Alhaitham's BIS. It is not recommended to pull this weapon specifically for Alhaitham unless you do not have Cinnabar Spindle.

Cinnabar Spindle

Alhaitham's recommended weapon if you have it. Provides large amounts of DEF% and increases skill damage based on defense. This weapon was made exclusively for Alhaitham.

Harbinger of Dawn

Best F2P weapon for those who missed out on Cinnabar Spindle. This weapon is a crit stat-stick with low base attack, which is perfect since Alhaitham's skill scales on defense.

Any 5* Sword

Any 5* sword is ok on Alhaitham. These weapons are stronger than Harbinger of Dawn when Alhaitham is played as an on-field carry.

Team Compositions

Zhongli Core

Alhaitham and Zhongli have excellent synergy, proving geo resonance, off field damage, and shielding. You can stick these two with most strong duos.

Chiori Core

Chiori and Alhaitham have incredible synergy, providing massive off-field damage, geo-resonance, and crystalize shields. You can stick these two with most strong duos, but make sure you choose elements that allow for crystalize.

Mono Geo

Alhaitham is a strong fit for mono geo teams. He provides energy for his team, and massive off-field damage due to being buffed by both Gorou and Zhongli.

Hyperbloom

Alhaitham can fit into most hyperbloom teams to add some extra geo damage. He can also buff elemental mastery with his burst, increasing hyperbloom damage.
