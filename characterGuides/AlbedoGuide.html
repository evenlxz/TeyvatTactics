Albedo Guide

Written by Evenlxz

General Info

Albedo is a 5 star off-field geo sword character in Genshin Impact. His primary role is as a sub-dps character that rarely interferes with reactions. Albedo is a decent choice in a wide variety of teams, although is often not the strongest choice.

Pros & Cons

Pros

-Completely off-field damage tied to elemental skill.

-Flexible team building.

-Synergy with Zhongli/Chiori for geo resonance.

-Elemental mastery buff on burst.

Cons

-Elemental skill can be easily destroyed by certain bosses.

-Flexible team building.

-Synergy with Zhongli/Chiori for geo resonance

-Generally overshadowed by Chiori.
