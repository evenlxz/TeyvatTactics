Albedo Guide

Written by: Evenlxz

General Info

Albedo is a 5-star geo sword character in Genshin Impact. His kit focuses on dealing off-field geo damage through his elemental skill. Albedo's skill, when placed, generates a burst of geo damage every 2 seconds when an enemy takes damage. Albedo's skill lasts a whopping 30 seconds, meaning it's not neccessary to switch back to him to continue damaging enemies. His burst does an instance of geo damage and buffs your teams elemental mastery. This makes Albedo a flexible damage dealer that can support most characters through off-field damage, geo-resonance, and elemental mastery buffing.

Pros & Cons

Pros

- Completely off-field damage tied to elemental skill.

- Flexible team building. Albedo can be put anywhere.

- Synergy with Zhongli/Chiori for geo-resonance.

- Elemental mastery buff on burst.

Cons

- Elemental skill can be easily destroyed by certain bosses.

- Potentially lower damage output than other support options.

- Needs at least geo-resonance to start doing noticable damage

- Generally overshadowed by Chiori.

How to Play

Albedo is simple character to play. Simply place your elemental skill and switch off him. If you are playing a team that can make use of elemental mastery, you should use his burst after his skill.

Be careful around large enemies or bosses, as placing your elemental skill inside of their models will cause the flower to instantly be destroyed. You can remedy this by placing Albedo's skill behind him, or holding his skill to get more precise placement.

Be aware that Albedo's skill DOES snapshot. Cast any buffs you want him before casting his skill.

Combo: Elemental Skill -> Elemental Burst (If required)

Weapons

Uraku Misugiri

Albedo's BIS. It is not recommended to pull this weapon specifically for Albedo unless you do not have Cinnabar Spindle.

Cinnabar Spindle

Albedo's recommended weapon if you have it. Provides large amounts of Def% and increases skill damage based on defense. This weapon was made exclusively for Albedo.

Harbinger of Dawn

Best F2P weapon for those who missed out on Cinnabar Spindle. This weapon is a crit stat-stick with low base attack, which is perfect since Albedo scales on defense.

Any 5* Sword

Any 5* sword is ok on Albedo. These weapons are stronger than Harbinger of Dawn when Albedo is played as an On-Field Carry.
